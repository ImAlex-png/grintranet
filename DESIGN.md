# Grintranet Design System

## Core Identity
A professional, modern, and high-tech interface designed for educational management. The aesthetic is rooted in **Glassmorphism** and **Nocturnal Depth**, providing a focused and premium experience.

## Colors
- **Primary:** `#38bdf8` (Sky Blue)
- **Secondary:** `#818cf8` (Indigo)
- **Background:** `#0f172a` (Slate 900)
- **Surface:** `rgba(30, 41, 59, 0.7)`
- **Error:** `#ef4444`
- **Success:** `#10b981`

## Typography
- **Headings:** Inter - SemiBold
- **Body:** Inter - Regular
- **Code:** JetBrains Mono

## Effects
- **Glassmorphism:** `backdrop-filter: blur(12px); border: 1px solid rgba(255, 255, 255, 0.1);`
- **Transitions:** `0.3s cubic-bezier(0.4, 0, 0.2, 1)`

## Shapes
- **Corner Radius:** 12px
